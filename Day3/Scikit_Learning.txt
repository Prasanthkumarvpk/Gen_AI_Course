Scikit-Learn Ante Enti?

Imagine:

Nuvvu oka HR company lo work chesthunav.

1000 candidates data undi.

Experience  Skills  Degree  Selected?
-------------------------------------
1 Year      5       B.Tech    No
3 Years     7       B.Tech    Yes
5 Years     8       M.Tech    Yes

Ippudu kotha candidate vachadu.

Experience = 4 Years
Skills = 7
Degree = B.Tech

Question:

Ee candidate select avuthada?

Manam manually guess cheyyachu.

Kani Machine Learning model thousands records chusi answer chepthundi.

Scikit-Learn ee models build cheyyadaniki use chestham.

Types Of Machine Learning
1. Supervised Learning

Teacher untadu.

Questions + Answers rendu untayi.

Example:

Experience   Salary
1            20000
2            30000
3            40000
4            50000

Question:

5 years experience unte salary entha?

Answer already data lo undi.

Idi Supervised Learning.

2. Unsupervised Learning

Teacher ledu.

Answers levu.

Example:

Shop customers data.

Customer A spends 500
Customer B spends 600
Customer C spends 5000
Customer D spends 5500

System chepthundi:

Group 1 = Normal Customers

Group 2 = Premium Customers

Idi Clustering.
